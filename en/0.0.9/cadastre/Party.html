Party¶

Natural Party¶

  1. Entering data manually using a form

Choose this option if you want to create a Natural Party using a form.

Natural Party is a natural person who has rights or who is subject to restrictions or responsibilities related to one or more Parcels.

  1. From another QGIS layer/table (setting a field mapping)

Choose this option to open a window that allows you to import data from a source table into the LADM_COL interesado_natural table.

If the field structure of input and target tables differs, you can set a field mapping to define field transformations and correspondence.

Legal Party¶

  1. Entering data manually using a form

Choose this option if you want to create a Legal Party using a form.

Legal Party is a legal entity that has rights, restrictions or responsibilities related to one or more Parcels.

  1. From another QGIS layer/table (setting a field mapping)

Choose this option to open a window that allows you to import data from a source table into the LADM_COL interesado_juridico table.

If the field structure of input and target tables differs, you can set a field mapping to define field transformations and correspondence.
